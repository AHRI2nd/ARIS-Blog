AniTime (the "App") takes your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information the App handles.

## 1. Information we do not collect

The App has no account or login system and does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email, phone number, location, or contacts.

## 2. Information stored only on your device

Your bookmarked anime list, notification settings, and display/language preferences are stored only in local storage on your device and are never sent to any server. Deleting the App deletes this data too.

## 3. Information collected automatically (crash reports)

The App uses Google Firebase Crashlytics to improve stability. If the App crashes, diagnostic information such as device model, OS version, app version, and the crash location is collected automatically. This information does not include personally identifiable information such as your name or email. See [Firebase's privacy policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) for details.

## 4. Retention period

Data stored on your device is deleted immediately when you delete the App. The retention period for diagnostic information collected by Firebase Crashlytics follows Google's own policy; see [Firebase's privacy policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) for details.

## 5. Your rights and how to exercise them

You have the right to request access to, correction of, deletion of, or suspension of processing of your personal information. Since the App does not separately collect or store personally identifiable information, there is generally no such information to access or correct. For requests such as deleting crash report data, please contact us at the address below and we will respond promptly.

## 6. Security measures

The App does not store personal information on its own servers, minimizing the risk of a data breach. Data stored on your device is protected by your OS's own security sandbox, and communication with Firebase uses an encrypted channel (HTTPS).

## 7. Ads and tracking

The App displays no ads and does not track users or combine data with third parties.

## 8. Source of broadcast data

Broadcast schedules and anime metadata are collected from public APIs such as Anissia, AniList, Tenrai, and Anime News Network. This process does not involve any personal information about you.

## 10. Changes to this policy
